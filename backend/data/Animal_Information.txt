Top 5 Information About Animals:

1. Animals are multicellular organisms that belong to the kingdom Animalia.
2. They are characterized by their ability to move, consume organic material, and respond to their environment.
3. Animals are classified into groups such as mammals, birds, reptiles, amphibians, fish, and insects.
4. Famous animals include lions, elephants, dolphins, eagles, and pandas, each known for unique traits.
5. Animals play crucial roles in ecosystems as predators, prey, pollinators, and decomposers.
