India, officially known as the Republic of India, is a country in South Asia. It is the seventh-largest country by land area and the second-most populous country in the world.

Key facts about India:
- Capital: New Delhi
- Currency: Indian Rupee (INR)
- Official languages: Hindi and English
- Population: Over 1.4 billion people
- Independence: August 15, 1947
- Government: Parliamentary democratic republic

India is known for its rich cultural heritage, diverse traditions, and rapidly growing technology sector. Major cities include Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Chennai, and Kolkata. The country is famous for its IT services, spices, Bollywood films, and historical monuments like the Taj Mahal.
